How the storm is formed

Storm is a term commonly used to describe a variety of atmospheric disturbances, from regular showers
and snowstorms to thunderstorms, wind, and wind-related disturbances such as storms, tornadoes, tropical cyclones,
and dust storms. A storm forms when a low pressure center develops with a surrounding high pressure system.
This combination of opposing forces can generate winds and form thunderclouds, such as cumulonimbus clouds.
